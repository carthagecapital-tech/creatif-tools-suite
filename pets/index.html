Why these apps

Most pet apps try to do too much (social network, marketplace, vet booking) and bury the actual care-tracking features behind a subscription. These apps focus on the one thing pet owners actually need: a clear, reliable record of your pet's health and care routine.

How to choose the right app

  • For homemade dog food, batch your recipes and save them in the calculator — easy to revisit and adjust as your dog's needs change.
  • Log medications as soon as you start them — past dose history is invaluable when talking to your vet.
  • Keep vaccination records in one place. They're often required for boarding, grooming, and travel.

Frequently asked questions
