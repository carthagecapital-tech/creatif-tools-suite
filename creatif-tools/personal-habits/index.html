Why these apps

Most habit apps turn into a social network and lose focus on the actual habit. These apps do one thing well: track your daily actions and visualize your progress. They work offline, save nothing to the cloud, and don't send you notifications trying to pull you back in.

How to choose the right app

  • Pick one habit to start with. Don't try to track five new things at once.
  • Daily check-ins matter more than weekly reviews. Build the 30-second habit of opening the app every day.
  • For the 75 Hard program specifically, the daily task list with a streak counter is a powerful motivator.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to be online?
No. The apps work offline once loaded. Your data stays on your device.
Is there a community or social features?
No. These are personal, private tools. The only 'community' is the people you choose to share your progress with.
