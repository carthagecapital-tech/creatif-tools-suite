Why these apps

Most couples argue about money not because of the money itself, but because of the tracking. A shared spreadsheet works until one of you forgets to update it. These apps give you a single source of truth that both partners can see and update. Local-first, no subscription, no bank account linking.

How to choose the right app

  • Track shared expenses as they happen, not at the end of the month — the math stays simple.
  • Set a monthly settlement day (like the 1st) so the 'who owes what' calculation has a clear cutoff.
  • Use the 'shared' vs 'personal' tag to keep individual purchases out of the joint ledger.

Frequently asked questions

Do both partners need to install the app?
No. The app is local-first, so each person can have their own copy on their device and you can sync verbally at the end of the month, or share the file via email/Dropbox.
Does it link to my bank account?
No. All data stays on your device. You'll enter shared expenses manually (it takes seconds).
