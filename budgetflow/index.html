    ⚠️ Important: Data Storage Notice

    All your financial data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

    • Data will not sync across devices or browsers.
    • Clearing your browser cache/history may delete all data.
    • Private/Incognito mode may not persist data after closing the window.

    We strongly recommend regularly exporting your data as a backup using the "Export Data" button above.
