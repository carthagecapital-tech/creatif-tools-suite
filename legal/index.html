Why these apps

Most legal document websites force you to sign up, upload your data, and pay monthly fees. These apps do the same job — generating legally-sound, state-aware documents — locally in your browser. No account, no subscription, your data never leaves your device.

How to use these apps

  • Pick the document you need (currently: vehicle bill of sale).
  • Select your state — the app loads the right disclosures automatically.
  • Fill in the parties, vehicle info, and sale details. Auto-save keeps your work.
  • Press ⌘P to print or save as PDF. The app strips UI chrome and outputs a clean, single-page document.
  • Sign and date. For state-specific requirements (notarization, witnesses), follow the disclosure shown.

Frequently asked questions

Do I need a lawyer to use these apps?
No. These apps generate standard, state-aware legal documents for private party sales. They are designed for routine transactions (vehicle sales, equipment sales). For complex legal situations (estate planning, business formation, contested transactions), consult a licensed attorney in your state.
Are the documents legally binding?
A bill of sale is a legal document when properly signed by both buyer and seller, dated, and witnessed (where required by state law). The app produces a complete, signed document that meets the requirements of all 50 states + DC. For state-specific requirements (notarization, additional forms), the app includes the relevant disclosure.
What vehicle types are supported?
Car, motorcycle, trailer, boat, RV, horse, and general equipment. Each vehicle type has its own conditional fields — boats require the Hull Identification Number, horses require breed and registration info, and so on.
