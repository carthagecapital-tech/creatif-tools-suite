Help
Quick setup — 5 steps
Step 01
Add Your First Item
Click New Item in the top-right or press ShiftN. Set the platform, source, buy price, and status. The profit preview updates live as you type.
Step 02
Track Inventory Status
Use Draft while sourcing, Listed when active. A yellow badge appears at 60 days listed, red at 90 days. Click Price on any listed row to reprice and log price history. Press 2 to jump to Inventory.
Step 03
Mark Items Sold
Click Sold on any row in Inventory. Enter the sale price, date, platform fee %, and outbound shipping. Profit and ROI calculate automatically. Supports Shipped, Refunded, and Donated statuses too.
Step 04
Review P&L and Reports
Sales (press 3) shows revenue, COGS, fees, and net profit by period. Reports (press 4) breaks down performance by platform, source, and category — and ranks your top 10 flips by ROI. The Dashboard calculator finds your max buy price for any target ROI.
Step 05
Export for Taxes
Go to Export (press 5) and download Sold Items CSV. Includes 16 columns your accountant needs: buy price, sale price, platform fees, shipping, net profit, and ROI per item. Use Export Backup in Settings for a full JSON backup you can restore on any device.
