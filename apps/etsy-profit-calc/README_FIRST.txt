EtsyCalc Pro v2.0 – Profit Calculator for Etsy Sellers

Thank you for purchasing EtsyCalc Pro! This tool helps you calculate
the true profit on your Etsy listings after all fees, costs, and expenses.

▸ WHAT YOU GET
  A single HTML file: EtsyCalc-Pro-v2.0.html
  No installation, no software – just open it in your browser.

▸ HOW TO OPEN
  Double-click the HTML file. It opens in your default browser.
  Works offline – no internet required after the first load.

▸ BROWSER SUPPORT
  Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (macOS 16+, iOS 16+).

▸ WHERE IS MY DATA STORED?
  Everything stays on your device, in your browser's local storage.
  Nothing is ever sent to the internet.
  To back up your data, use the Export CSV feature inside the app.

▸ GETTING STARTED
  1. Open the file.
  2. Enter a product name, price, shipping, and material costs.
  3. See your net profit and fee breakdown instantly.
  4. Click Save Listing to keep the result.
  5. Switch to the Saved Listings view to compare all your products.
