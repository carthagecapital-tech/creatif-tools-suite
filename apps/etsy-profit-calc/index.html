§ 00getting started

Welcome to EtsyCalc Pro — a profit calculator designed specifically for Etsy sellers.

To get started: Enter your product name, selling price, and costs on the Calculator page. The app shows your true profit after all Etsy fees in real time. Click Save listing to store calculations for later comparison.

Supported browsers: Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Safari (macOS/iOS 16+). All data is stored locally on your device.

§ 01how to use the calculator

The Etsy Profit Calculator helps you determine the true profitability of your listings by accounting for all Etsy fees, material costs, shipping, and packaging expenses.

  • Enter your selling price — the price you list the item for
  • Add your costs — shipping, materials, and packaging per item
  • Select your market — country affects fee structure and currency
  • Toggle Etsy Ads — if you use offsite ads, enable this fee
  • Set target margin — the calculator suggests a price to hit your goal

All calculations update in real-time as you type. Save listings to compare scenarios later.

§ 02etsy fee reference

Listing FeeCharged when you publish or renew a listing
Transaction Fee6.5% of the sale price including shipping
Payment ProcessingVaries by country (typically 3-4% + fixed fee)
Offsite Ads Fee15% for most sellers; 12% if annual sales exceed $10k (optional)
Currency Conversion2.5% if listing currency differs from bank currency

Note: EU listing fees are currently €0.18. Verify current rates before pricing. Fee structures change frequently — always confirm at etsy.com/fees.

§ 04data & privacy

Your data stays on your device. All calculations and saved listings are stored locally in your browser. Nothing is sent to any server or cloud service.

Back up your data: Use the Export CSV feature regularly to back up your listings. You can open the CSV file in Excel or Google Sheets.

Clearing browser data will erase your saved listings. Always export before clearing cookies or site data.
