1. WHAT THIS APP DOES

This app helps resellers quickly calculate profit after all
platform fees, processing fees, shipping costs, taxes, and
discounts. It supports Poshmark, Mercari, Depop, and a
Custom option where you define your own fee structure.

You can save unlimited listings, compare two platforms
side‑by‑side, and see charts of your performance over time.

====================================================
2. HOW TO OPEN AND KEEP YOUR DATA SAFE

 • Double‑click the HTML file. It opens in your browser.
 • There is no installer, no account, no internet needed.
 • All your data is stored in your browser's local storage.
 • Clearing your browser's cookies/history WILL DELETE all
   your saved listings and settings.
 • Protect your work:
     1. Go to Settings → Export All Data.
     2. Save the .json file somewhere safe (Desktop, cloud).
     3. To restore, go to Settings → Import Data and choose
        that file.

3. CALCULATOR VIEW IN DETAIL

Fields:
  Item Name          – required; name of the item sold.
  Cost of Goods (COG) – what you paid to acquire the item.
  Sold Price          – final price the buyer paid.
  Platform            – choose Poshmark, Mercari, Depop, or Custom.
  Shipping Cost       – actual shipping label cost (for seller).
  Shipping Paid By    – toggle: Buyer (they pay shipping) or Seller.
  Tax Rate            – sales tax percentage (e.g., 8.25 for 8.25%).
  Discount / Promo    – any discount amount you gave.
  Category Tag        – optional label (Clothing, Electronics, etc.).
  Notes               – any extra notes.

When you select "Custom" as the platform, four extra fields
appear: Commission Rate (%), Fixed Platform Fee ($),
Processing Rate (%), and Processing Fixed Fee ($).
Fill these in with your own platform's fee structure.

The app updates results LIVE as you type.
The fee breakdown bar shows the split visually.

SAVE BUTTON:
  "Calculate & Save" stores the listing. If you are editing
  an existing listing, the button changes to "Update Listing".

RESET FORM:
  Clears all fields and returns to defaults from Settings.

COMPARE BUTTON:
  Sends the current item's cost and name to the Compare view
  so you can quickly compare platforms.

4. PLATFORM FEE REFERENCE

(The built‑in presets are accurate as of early 2025. Always
double‑check the platform's website for the latest rates.)

Poshmark:
  • Under $15 sale: flat $2.95 fee.
  • $15 and above: 20% of the sold price.
  • Shipping typically paid by buyer (label ≈ $7.97).
  • No separate payment processing fee.

Mercari:
  • 10% flat selling fee.
  • No separate payment processing fee for sellers.
  • Shipping can be paid by buyer or seller.

Depop:
  • 0% seller commission (US/UK).
  • Payment processing: ~3.3% + $0.45 per transaction.
  • Shipping can be paid by buyer or seller.

Custom:
  • You set your own commission %, fixed fee, processing %,
    and processing fixed fee.
  • Great for your own website, Facebook Marketplace, etc.

5. SAVED LISTINGS VIEW

• Table shows all saved listings.
• Search bar filters by name, category, notes, or platform.
• Category chips and platform chips filter the list.
• Click any column header (Item, Platform, COG, Sold, etc.)
  to sort the table.
• Each row has three buttons:
    Edit – loads the listing into the Calculator for editing.
    Dup  – creates a copy with "(copy)" added to the name.
    Del  – deletes the listing (asks for confirmation).

BATCH ACTIONS:
  • Check one or more rows.
  • Use "Select All", "Delete Selected", or "Duplicate Selected".
  • The batch bar appears when items are selected.

EXPORT CSV:
  • Downloads all currently visible (filtered) listings as a
    CSV file. You can open it in Excel, Google Sheets, etc.

PAGINATION:
  • Shows 10 listings per page.
  • Use the arrows at the bottom to navigate pages.

====================================================
6. COMPARE VIEW

 • Enter the Cost of Goods once at the top.
 • Optionally, set an Item Name (filled automatically if you
   used the "Compare" button from the Calculator).
 • Choose two different platforms (Platform A and Platform B).
 • Enter the selling price, shipping cost, shipping payer,
   tax rate, and discount for each side independently.
 • The net profit, margin, ROI, total fees, and break‑even
   price are shown below each platform.
 • A "winner" banner tells you which platform gives more profit
   (or if it's a tie).

Tip: Use this view before listing a high‑value item.

7. ANALYTICS VIEW

Top row – six summary cards:
  • Total Listings
  • Total Net Profit
  • Average Margin
  • Best Platform (by total net profit)
  • Best Category (by average profit margin)
  • Total Fees Paid

Four charts:
  1. Profit by Platform – bar chart, total net profit per platform.
  2. Fee Breakdown by Platform – grouped bars showing average
     platform fee, processing, shipping, and tax per platform.
  3. Category Performance – horizontal bar chart of average
     margin per category.
  4. Monthly Profit Trend – line chart over time.

Charts only appear after you have saved at least one listing.

8. SETTINGS VIEW

Preferences:
  • Default Platform – pre‑selected in the Calculator.
  • Default Shipping Paid By – Buyer or Seller.
  • Default Tax Rate – automatically filled in for new calculations.
  • Default Shipping Cost – automatically filled in.

Calculator Defaults:
  • Default Discount Amount – pre‑filled discount.
  • Default Category – automatically selected category tag.

Appearance:
  • Accent Hue – change the theme color (slide to any hue 0‑360).
  • Reset – returns to the default teal color.

9. CUSTOM FEE PROFILES

In Settings → Custom Fee Builder you can create named profiles
with your own fee structure (e.g., "My Boutique", "Facebook 5%").

Each profile has a name, commission rate %, fixed fee, processing
rate %, and processing fixed fee.

You can edit or delete profiles at any time.
These profiles are not automatically applied; you select
"Custom" as the platform in the Calculator, then fill in the
numbers manually. (Profiles serve as a template reference.)

10. BACKUP, IMPORT, AND CLEAR DATA

Export All Data:
  Creates a .json file containing all your listings, settings,
  and custom profiles. Save this file somewhere safe.

Import Data:
  Replaces all current data with the data from a backup file.
  You'll see a warning with the file name before import.
  Always export a current backup before importing.

Clear All Data:
  Deletes everything (listings, settings, profiles) permanently.
  Use with caution. Export a backup first.

13. TROUBLESHOOTING

"I don't see my saved listings after restarting my computer."
  → You may have cleared your browser data, or you're using
    private/incognito mode. Always export a backup.

"The app looks broken or colors are wrong."
  → Update your browser to the latest version. If you're on
    an older Mac (Safari 15), some colors won't work.
    Use Chrome or Firefox instead.

"I got a 'Storage full' message."
  → Export your data, then delete old listings. The app can
    store thousands of listings, but your browser has a limit.

"Changes I made didn't save."
  → If you are editing a listing, you must click "Update
    Listing" (or "Calculate & Save" for a new one) to save.
    Navigating away without saving will warn you.
