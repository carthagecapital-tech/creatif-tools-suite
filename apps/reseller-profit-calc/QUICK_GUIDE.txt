====================================================
 QUICK GUIDE — Resell Profit Calculator Pro v2.2
====================================================

GET STARTED IN 3 MINUTES

1. CALCULATOR TAB
   • Enter the item name (required).
   • Fill in:
        Cost of Goods      – what you paid
        Sold Price         – what the buyer paid
        Platform           – Poshmark / Mercari / Depop / Custom
        Shipping Cost      – what shipping actually costs you
        Shipping Paid By   – toggle if the buyer or you pay
        Tax Rate           – sales tax percentage you collect
        Discount           – any promo or discount you gave
        Category           – optional, for reporting
   • The results update live as you type.
   • Click "Calculate & Save" to store the listing.

2. SAVED LISTINGS TAB
   • View every listing you've saved.
   • Search, filter by platform or category, sort columns.
   • Select multiple rows for batch delete / duplicate.
   • Export all saved listings to a CSV spreadsheet.

3. COMPARE TAB
   • Enter the same item and cost once.
   • Choose two different platforms.
   • See side‑by‑side which one gives you more profit.

4. ANALYTICS TAB
   • Summary cards for total profit, best platform, etc.
   • Bar charts and trend line (monthly profit).

5. SETTINGS TAB
   • Change default platform, shipping payer, tax rate.
   • Build custom fee profiles for your own website.
   • Export full backup (JSON) or import one.
   • Clear all data (with confirmation).

6. HELP TAB
   • Full fee reference, formulas, keyboard shortcuts.

TIPS:
   • Use the Compare tool before listing an item on a new platform.
   • Export a CSV file for your accounting or tax records.
   • Regularly backup your data (Settings → Export All Data).

Keyboard shortcuts:
   Ctrl/Cmd + K      → Focus search box
   Ctrl/Cmd + P      → Print current view
   Ctrl/Cmd + S      → Calculate & Save
   Escape            → Close modal dialog
