─────────────────────────────────────────
NAVIGATION
─────────────────────────────────────────
The app has 6 sections accessible from the left sidebar:

  01 Calculator  →  Quote builder & cost estimator
  02 Jobs        →  Saved quotes & bookings
  03 Clients     →  Client roster & history
  04 Analytics   →  Business performance metrics
  05 Settings    →  Defaults & data management
  06 Help        →  Formulas & reference

─────────────────────────────────────────
01 — CALCULATOR
─────────────────────────────────────────
The main quoting tool. All results update in real time.

INPUTS:
  Client Name       Required to save the job
  Job Address       Physical address of the job
  Cleaning Type     Standard / Deep / Move-In / Move-Out / Post-Construction
  Frequency         One-time / Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly
  Square Footage    Property size in sq ft
  Bedrooms          Number of bedrooms
  Bathrooms         Number of bathrooms
  Extra Rooms       Office, basement, sunroom, etc.
  Cleaners          Number of cleaners on the job
  Est. Hours        Auto-calculated from room count, or set manually
  Labor Rate        Your hourly rate per cleaner ($/hr)
  Supplies Cost     Cost of cleaning supplies for this job ($)
  Drive Distance    One-way miles from your location to the job
  Drive Time        One-way drive time in minutes
  Discount          Optional discount amount ($)
  Job Status        Quote / Confirmed / Completed / Cancelled
  Notes             Internal notes for this job

HOURS MODE:
  Auto  → Hours calculated automatically based on cleaning type,
           square footage, bedrooms, bathrooms, and extra rooms
  Manual → Override with your own estimated hours

OUTPUTS — QUOTE RESULTS:
  Quoted Price      What to charge the client
  Total Cost        Labor + supplies + mileage + drive time cost
  Net Profit        Quoted price minus total cost
  Profit Margin     Net profit as a % of quoted price
  Suggested Price   Minimum price to hit your target margin (set in Settings)

COST BREAKDOWN BAR:
  Visual bar showing: Labor | Supplies | Mileage | Drive Time

ACTIONS:
  Save Job          Saves to your jobs list (client name required)
  Reset Form        Clears all inputs back to defaults
  Print Quote       Prints a clean client-facing quote

─────────────────────────────────────────
02 — JOBS
─────────────────────────────────────────
All saved quotes and bookings in one place.

FEATURES:
  - Search by client name or address
  - Filter by job status (All / Quote / Confirmed / Completed / Cancelled)
  - Sort by any column
  - Click any row to load the job back into the Calculator
  - Delete individual or multiple jobs
  - Export all jobs to CSV (Ctrl + E)

─────────────────────────────────────────
03 — CLIENTS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Auto-built from your saved jobs. Every unique client name
gets a client profile showing:

  - Total jobs for that client
  - Total revenue generated
  - Average job value
  - Full job history

No manual data entry required — clients are created
automatically when you save jobs.

─────────────────────────────────────────
04 — ANALYTICS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Visual overview of your business performance.

METRICS INCLUDE:
  - Total revenue across all completed jobs
  - Average profit margin across all jobs
  - Job count by status
  - Revenue over time
  - Top clients by revenue
  - Most profitable job types

─────────────────────────────────────────
05 — SETTINGS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Set your defaults so the calculator pre-fills correctly
every time you open it.

DEFAULTS:
  Default Labor Rate ($/hr)     Your standard hourly rate per cleaner
  Mileage Rate ($/mile)         IRS standard or your own rate (default $0.67)
  Target Margin (%)             Your minimum acceptable profit margin
  Default Supplies Cost ($)     Your typical per-job supply cost
  Default Drive Distance (mi)   Your typical one-way drive distance

APPEARANCE:
  Accent Hue    Customize the app's accent color

DATA MANAGEMENT:
  JSON Export   Back up all jobs and settings to a .json file
  JSON Import   Restore from a previous backup
  Clear All     Permanently delete all jobs and reset settings
                (cannot be undone — export first)

─────────────────────────────────────────
FORMULAS REFERENCE
─────────────────────────────────────────
  Labor Cost        cleaners × labor rate × hours
  Mileage Cost      drive distance × 2 × mileage rate
  Drive Time Cost   cleaners × labor rate × (drive minutes × 2 ÷ 60)
  Total Cost        labor + supplies + mileage + drive time
  Quoted Price      total cost ÷ (1 − target margin ÷ 100)
  Net Profit        quoted price − total cost − discount
  Profit Margin     (net profit ÷ quoted price) × 100
  Suggested Price   minimum price to hit your target margin

─────────────────────────────────────────
DATA & PRIVACY
─────────────────────────────────────────
  - All data stored in your browser's localStorage
  - No data sent to any server
  - Works 100% offline
  - Back up regularly: Settings → JSON Export
  - Clearing browser cache will erase saved jobs
