─────────────────────────────────────────
NAVIGATION
─────────────────────────────────────────
The app has 6 sections accessible from the left sidebar:

  01 Calculator  →  Main profit calculator
  02 Products    →  Saved product catalog
  03 Compare     →  Side-by-side product comparison
  04 Analytics   →  Profitability charts and insights
  05 Settings    →  Defaults and preferences
  06 Help        →  Fee tables and formula reference

Use the search bar (Ctrl + K) to jump between saved products.

─────────────────────────────────────────
01 — CALCULATOR
─────────────────────────────────────────
The core tool. Fill in your product inputs and all results
update in real time.

INPUTS:
  Product Name    Required if you want to save the product
  ASIN            Optional reference field
  Category        Determines the Amazon referral fee rate
  Size Tier       Standard Small / Standard Large / Large Bulky / Extra Large
  Unit Weight     In lbs — used to calculate FBA fulfillment fee
  Product Cost    Your per-unit cost (COGS), landed
  Prep Cost       Per-unit prep or labeling cost
  Inbound Ship    Cost per unit to ship to an FBA warehouse
  Selling Price   Your Amazon listing price
  Monthly Volume  Expected units sold per month
  Storage Volume  Cubic feet per unit (length × width × height ÷ 1728)
  Storage Month   Jan-Sep uses standard rate / Oct-Dec uses peak rate
  Target ROI      Used to calculate your suggested selling price

OUTPUTS — PROFIT ANALYSIS:
  Net Profit      Selling price minus all fees and costs (per unit)
  Margin          Net profit as a % of selling price
  ROI             Net profit as a % of your total cost
  Amazon's Cut    Referral fee + FBA fulfillment fee + storage fee
  Your Cost       Product cost + prep cost + inbound shipping
  Monthly Profit  Net profit × monthly volume

COST BREAKDOWN BAR:
  Visual bar showing exactly where your money goes:
  COGS | Referral Fee | FBA Fee | Storage | Prep & Ship

FEE BREAKDOWN TABLE:
  Referral Fee          → % of selling price (varies by category)
  FBA Fulfillment Fee   → Based on size tier and unit weight
  Storage Fee           → Based on volume and storage month
  Total Amazon Fees     → Sum of all Amazon charges
  Total Cost            → COGS + prep + inbound shipping
  Total Deductions      → Total Amazon Fees + Total Cost
  Net Profit            → Selling Price − Total Deductions

SUGGESTED PRICE & BREAK-EVEN:
  Suggested Price        → Minimum price to hit your target ROI
  Break-Even Price       → Minimum price to avoid a loss
  Break-Even Units/Month → Units needed per month to cover storage costs

PROFITABILITY BADGE:
  STRONG   Margin > 40%
  HEALTHY  Margin between 20% and 40%
  THIN     Margin > 0% but below 20%
  LOSS     Net profit is negative — selling at a loss

ACTIONS:
  Save Product    → Saves to your product catalog (requires a name)
  Reset Form      → Clears all inputs back to defaults
  Send to Compare → Loads this product into the Compare view

─────────────────────────────────────────
02 — PRODUCTS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Your saved product catalog. All products are stored locally
in your browser.

FEATURES:
  - Search by name or ASIN
  - Sort by any column (name, margin, ROI, profit, fees, date)
  - Filter by profitability badge (All / Loss / Thin / Healthy / Strong)
  - Click any product row to load it back into the Calculator
  - Select multiple products and delete them
  - Export all products to CSV (Ctrl + E)

SUMMARY BAR (bottom):
  Shows total saved products, average margin, and average ROI
  across your entire catalog.

─────────────────────────────────────────
03 — COMPARE
─────────────────────────────────────────
Compare two products side by side to see which is more profitable.

HOW TO USE:
  Option A: Use "Send to Compare" from the Calculator view
  Option B: Manually enter two sets of product inputs directly
            in the Compare view

Both products calculate simultaneously. Results show net profit,
margin, ROI, total fees, and total cost for each side.
Export the comparison to CSV using the Export button.

─────────────────────────────────────────
04 — ANALYTICS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Visual overview of your saved product catalog.

CHARTS INCLUDE:
  - Margin distribution across your products
  - ROI distribution across your products
  - Profitability badge breakdown (Strong / Healthy / Thin / Loss)
  - Average margin and average ROI across all saved products

Useful for spotting which products are underperforming and
where to focus your sourcing efforts.

─────────────────────────────────────────
FORMULAS REFERENCE
─────────────────────────────────────────
  Referral Fee (flat)    MAX(price × rate, minimum fee)
  Referral Fee (tiered)  (threshold × tier1) + MAX(0, (price − threshold) × tier2)
  FBA Fee                Looked up from size tier + weight bracket table
  Storage Fee            Storage rate × storage volume (ft³)
  Total Amazon Fees      Referral + FBA + Storage
  Total Cost             Product cost + prep cost + inbound shipping
  Total Deductions       Total Amazon Fees + Total Cost
  Net Profit             Selling price − Total Deductions
  Profit Margin          (Net Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100
  ROI                    (Net Profit ÷ Total Cost) × 100
  Break-Even Price       Total Cost ÷ (1 − effective rate) + FBA fee + storage fee
  Suggested Price        (Total Cost × (1 + target ROI ÷ 100) + FBA + storage) ÷ (1 − effective rate)

─────────────────────────────────────────
DATA & PRIVACY
─────────────────────────────────────────
- All data is stored in your browser's localStorage
- No data is sent to any server
- Works 100% offline after the file is downloaded
- To back up your data: Settings → Export Data (JSON)
  or Ctrl + E to export a CSV
- Clearing browser cache will erase saved products —
  export regularly as a precaution
