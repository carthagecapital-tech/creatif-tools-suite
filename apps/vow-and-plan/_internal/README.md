# Vow & Plan — Premium Wedding Planner

**Version:** 4.1 | **Commercial Grade** | **100% Offline** | **PWA Ready**

---

## What You're Getting

Vow & Plan is a complete, offline-first wedding planning application that runs entirely in your browser. No internet connection required after initial load. No subscriptions. No data tracking. Your wedding data stays private on your device.

## Features (25 Modules)

- **Dashboard** — Wedding countdown, task progress, budget summary, guest RSVP overview
- **Timeline & Checklist** — Smart task lists with subtasks, timeframes, and day-of schedule
- **Guest List** — Full guest management with RSVPs, meal choices, allergies, plus-ones
- **Households** — Invitation tracking, postage costs, RSVP management
- **Budget & Expenses** — Category-based budgeting with charts and expense tracking
- **Vendors** — Contact management, payment tracking, ratings
- **Quote Comparison** — Side-by-side vendor quote evaluation
- **Seating Chart** — Visual SVG-based drag-and-drop seating arrangement
- **Wedding Party** — Bridal party management with attire tracking
- **Attire Tracker** — Dress, suit, ring tracking with status and due dates
- **Ceremony Builder** — Build your ceremony script step by step
- **Speeches & Toasts** — Organize speeches with timing and notes
- **Music Playlist** — Curate songs for each wedding moment
- **Photo Shot List** — Essential wedding photography checklist
- **Gift Registry** — Track gifts and thank-you note status
- **Transportation** — Guest shuttle and vehicle arrangements
- **Accommodations** — Hotel room block management
- **Notes** — Organized note-taking with categories
- **Moodboard** — Upload and arrange inspiration images
- **Honeymoon Planner** — Trip planning with checklist
- **Post-Wedding Tasks** — Thank-you notes, name changes, vendor reviews
- **Printables** — Export seating chart and shot list to PDF
- **Settings** — PIN protection, themes, backup/restore
- **Search** — Global search across all data
- **Undo** — Full undo system with history

## Installation

### Option 1: Open Directly
1. Extract the downloaded ZIP file
2. Double-click `index.html`
3. Start planning!

### Option 2: Install as App (PWA)
**Chrome/Edge:**
1. Open `index.html` in your browser
2. Click the install icon in the address bar (looks like a computer with a down arrow)
3. Follow the prompts to install

**Safari (iOS):**
1. Open `index.html` in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"

**Chrome (Android):**
1. Open `index.html` in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Tap "Add to Home screen"

## Data Privacy

- **100% Offline:** All data is stored locally on your device using IndexedDB
- **No Cloud Sync:** Your data never leaves your device
- **No Tracking:** No analytics, no cookies, no third-party services
- **PIN Protection:** Optional 4-digit PIN to protect your data
- **Backup & Restore:** Export your data as a JSON file for safekeeping

## Data Backup

**Important:** Since all data is stored locally, we strongly recommend regular backups.

1. Go to **Settings**
2. Click **Export Data** to save a backup JSON file
3. Store the backup file in a safe location (cloud storage, USB drive, etc.)
4. To restore, click **Import Data** and select your backup file
