Thank you for your purchase! This file tells you
everything you need to get started.

WHAT YOU HAVE
─────────────
You've received a single HTML file:
  HandmadePricingCalculator.html

That's the whole app. No installation, no
software, no internet required after the first
time you open it.

HOW TO OPEN
───────────
Double‑click the HTML file. It will open in
your default web browser (Chrome, Edge,
Firefox, or Safari).

If your computer asks which program to use,
choose any modern browser.

BROWSER SUPPORT
───────────────
• Google Chrome  (any recent version)
• Microsoft Edge (any recent version)
• Mozilla Firefox (any recent version)
• Apple Safari    (version 14 or newer)

The app works fully offline — it does NOT
send any data over the internet.

WHERE IS MY DATA?
─────────────────
Everything you type stays on YOUR computer,
inside your browser's private storage
(localStorage). No one else can see it.

To keep your data safe:
  → Export a backup regularly using the
    "Export JSON" button in Settings.
  → That backup file is a safety net if you
    ever clear your browser data.

IMPORTANT NOTES
───────────────
• If you use the app in a private/incognito
  browsing window, your data WILL be erased
  when you close that window.

• Having the app open in two browser tabs
  at once can cause data conflicts. We
  recommend using only ONE tab.

• Your browser may warn you about "unsaved
  changes" when you close the page — that's
  the app reminding you to save.
