WHAT YOU HAVE
─────────────
A single HTML file: Food_Business_Cost_Calculator.html

No installation. No subscription. No internet required
after download.

HOW TO OPEN IT
──────────────
1. Download the file to your computer
2. Double-click it — it opens in your browser
3. Start using it immediately

RECOMMENDED BROWSERS
────────────────────
Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge (any modern browser)

YOUR DATA
─────────
All data is saved locally in your browser (localStorage).
Nothing is sent to any server. If you clear your browser
data, your saved recipes will be cleared too.

  TIP: Export your recipes to CSV regularly as a backup.
       Use the "Export CSV" button in the Saved Recipes view
       or press Ctrl + E.

MILEAGE RATE
────────────
The calculator uses the IRS standard mileage rate for
market trip cost calculations. This rate changes annually.
The built-in rate was accurate as of May 2026. Check the
current rate at irs.gov and update it in Settings if needed.
