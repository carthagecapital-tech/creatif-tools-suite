  CLEANPRO PRICER — QUICK GUIDE
  Up and running in under 2 minutes
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — OPEN THE APP
─────────────────────────────────────────
Double-click CleanPro_Pricer.html
It opens in your browser. No setup needed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 2 — ENTER YOUR JOB
─────────────────────────────────────────
Fill in the Calculator (01 calculator):

  Client Name       → Required to save the job
  Job Address       → Location of the cleaning job
  Cleaning Type     → Standard / Deep / Move-In / Move-Out / Post-Construction
  Frequency         → One-time / Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly
  Square Footage    → Size of the property
  Bedrooms          → Number of bedrooms
  Bathrooms         → Number of bathrooms
  Extra Rooms       → Any additional spaces (office, basement, etc.)
  Cleaners          → How many cleaners on the job
  Est. Hours        → Manual or auto-calculated from room count
  Labor Rate ($/hr) → Your hourly rate per cleaner
  Supplies Cost     → Cost of supplies for this job
  Drive Distance    → One-way miles to the job
  Drive Time        → One-way drive time in minutes
  Job Status        → Quote / Confirmed / Completed / Cancelled
  Notes             → Any job-specific notes

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 3 — READ YOUR QUOTE
─────────────────────────────────────────
Results update instantly as you type:

  Quoted Price    → What to charge the client
  Total Cost      → Your actual cost (labor + supplies + mileage + drive)
  Net Profit      → What you keep after costs
  Profit Margin   → % of quoted price that is profit
  Suggested Price → Minimum price to hit your target margin

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 4 — SAVE THE JOB
─────────────────────────────────────────
Click "Save Job" or press Ctrl + S.
View all saved jobs under 02 jobs.

─────────────────────────────────────────
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
─────────────────────────────────────────
  Ctrl + S  →  Save job
  Ctrl + E  →  Export all jobs to CSV
  Ctrl + K  →  Focus search bar
  Ctrl + P  →  Print current view
