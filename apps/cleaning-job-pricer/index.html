§ 01formula reference

Hour Estimate: baseHours = (sqFt / 1000) × 1.5 × typeMult + bathrooms × 0.5 × typeMult + max(0, bedrooms − 1) × 0.25 + extraRoomHours × typeMult, rounded up to 0.25h

Labor Cost: cleaners × rate × hours

Mileage: miles × 2 × mileageRate (round trip)

Drive Time Cost: cleaners × rate × (minutes × 2 / 60)

Total Cost: labor + supplies + mileage + drive time

Base Price: totalCost / (1 − targetMargin/100)

Quoted: basePrice × (1 − frequencyDiscount/100)

Profit: quotedPrice − totalCost

Margin: (profit / quotedPrice) × 100
