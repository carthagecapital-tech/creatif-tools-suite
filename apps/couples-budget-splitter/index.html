§ 04About

splitter / budget v1.4.3 — A couples finance tool for tracking shared expenses, splitting bills fairly, and saving together.

All data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to the internet.

Works offline. No account needed. No subscriptions.

§ 01Getting Started

Welcome to splitter / budget, a finance tool for couples who want to track shared expenses and split bills fairly.

To get started:

  • Go to Settings and set your names and monthly incomes
  • Add your first expense from the Expenses page
  • Choose how to split each expense (even, income-based, custom, or fixed)
  • Watch your Dashboard update with real-time balance tracking

All data is saved automatically to your device. Nothing is sent to the internet.

§ 02Split Methods Explained

50/50 Even Split: Both partners pay exactly half, regardless of income.

Income-Based Proportional: Each pays proportional to their monthly income.

Custom Percentage: You set exact percentages for each partner.

Fixed Amount: Each partner pays a specific dollar amount.

§ 03How Balance Tracking Works

For each expense, the app calculates who paid versus who should have paid. The running balance shows who owes who across all expenses and subscriptions.

§ 04Subscriptions

Subscriptions are always split 50/50. Track who pays each recurring bill and see the monthly equivalent cost.

§ 05Tips for Couples

  • Be consistent: Log expenses regularly
  • Review monthly: Check Analytics to understand spending
  • Set goals together: Use Goals for shared savings
  • Export regularly: Back up your data by exporting CSV files
