==========================================================
 SPLITTER / BUDGET – Quick Start Guide
==========================================================

FIRST STEPS
--------------------------------
1. Open the HTML file.
2. Click on "Settings" in the left menu.
3. Change the partner names to yours.
4. Set each partner’s monthly income (used for fair splits).
5. Click any other tab – your settings are saved automatically.

ADDING YOUR FIRST EXPENSE
--------------------------------
1. Click "Expenses" in the left menu.
2. Fill in:
      • Name (e.g. "Grocery run")
      • Amount (in dollars/pounds/euros etc.)
      • Who paid (you or your partner)
      • Date (defaults to today)
      • Split type (see below)
3. Click "+ Add Expense"

Your expense is saved immediately. The Dashboard updates
automatically.

SPLIT TYPES (QUICK EXPLANATION)
--------------------------------
• 50/50 Even – both pay half.
• Income-Based – split according to your incomes.
• Custom % – you set each partner's percentage.
• Fixed Amount – each pays a specific dollar figure.

ADDING A RECURRING SUBSCRIPTION
--------------------------------
1. Click "Subscriptions" in the menu.
2. Add the name, cost per billing cycle, who pays, and cycle.
3. The app shows the monthly equivalent cost and splits it
  50/50.

VIEWING YOUR BALANCE
--------------------------------
The Dashboard shows who owes who. A positive balance means
partner 2 owes partner 1 (and vice‑versa). It’s that simple!

BACKING UP YOUR DATA
--------------------------------
Go to Settings → "Export All Data" (or "Export Expenses Only").
Save the CSV file somewhere safe. To restore, use "Import
Expenses" in the same section.

That’s all you need to start! For more details, see the
User Guide.
