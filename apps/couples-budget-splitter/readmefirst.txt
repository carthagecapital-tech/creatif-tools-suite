Thank you for purchasing Splitter / Budget!
This tool helps you and your partner track shared expenses,
split bills fairly, and save together – all in one simple file.

WHAT YOU GOT
--------------------------------
The Zip file contains:
  • Splitter-Budget-v1.4.1.html   (the app)
  • readmefirst.txt              (this file)
  • quickguide.txt               (quick start)
  • userguide.txt                (full guide)

HOW TO OPEN THE APP
--------------------------------
1. Extract all files from the Zip to a folder on your computer.
2. Double-click the HTML file (Splitter-Budget-v1.4.1.html).
   It will open in your default browser.

No installation needed. No internet required after the first
open (the app works completely offline).

SUPPORTED BROWSERS
--------------------------------
• Google Chrome (recommended)
• Microsoft Edge
• Mozilla Firefox
• Safari (macOS)

IMPORTANT SAFARI NOTE: Safari may not save data when used in
Private Browsing mode. Please use a regular browser window.

PRIVACY
--------------------------------
All your data stays on your device. Nothing is sent to the
internet. The app uses your browser’s local storage.
