§ 01How to Use

Calculator: Enter your item details, choose a platform, and see real-time profit calculations. Click "Calculate & Save" to store the listing. Toggle shipping payer and adjust tax rates to match your situation.

Saved Listings: Browse all your saved calculations. Search, filter by category or platform, sort columns, and use batch actions. Export to CSV anytime.

Compare: Side-by-side platform comparison using the same item. See which platform maximizes your profit before you list.

Analytics: Visual breakdowns of your reselling performance across platforms and categories.

Settings: Customize defaults, build custom fee profiles, and manage your data.

§ 03Formula Reference

Gross Profit = Sold Price - Cost of Goods
Platform Fees = (Sold Price * Commission Rate) + Fixed Fee
Exception: Poshmark uses $2.95 flat for sales under $15, 20% for $15+
Taxable Amount = Sold Price + (Shipping if buyer pays)
Processing Fees = (Taxable Amount * Processing Rate) + Processing Fixed Fee
Shipping Cost = Shipping if seller pays, else $0
Tax Amount = Sold Price * (Tax Rate / 100)
Total Fees = Platform + Processing + Shipping + Tax + Discount
Net Profit = Gross Profit - Total Fees
Profit Margin = (Net Profit / Sold Price) * 100
ROI = (Net Profit / Cost of Goods) * 100
Break-Even = Cost of Goods / (1 - Total Fee Rate)

§ 04Tips for Resellers

  • Factor in all fees before pricing — a $30 sale on Poshmark nets $24, but on Mercari it's $27 (10% flat).
  • Use the Compare tool before listing to find the most profitable platform for each item.
  • Shipping paid by buyer increases your net but may deter buyers; test both strategies.
  • Track categories to discover your most profitable niches over time.
  • Factor in your time — sourcing, photographing, listing, and shipping all have costs.
  • Use custom fee profiles for your own website or boutique with different fee structures.

§ 05Supported Browsers

Works in Chrome, Edge, Firefox (desktop), and Safari 16+ (macOS Ventura or newer). Safari 15 and older will display incorrectly due to missing CSS color support. All data stays on your device — nothing is sent to the internet. Your listings are stored in your browser's local storage, so clearing browser data will erase them. Export a backup regularly.
