  AMAZON FBA PROFIT CALCULATOR — QUICK GUIDE
  Up and running in under 2 minutes
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 1 — OPEN THE APP
─────────────────────────────────────────
Double-click Amazon_FBA_Profit_Calculator.html
It opens in your browser. No setup needed.

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 2 — ENTER YOUR PRODUCT
─────────────────────────────────────────
Fill in the Calculator (left panel → 01 calculator):

  Product Name    → Your product title
  ASIN            → Optional, for your reference
  Category        → Select your Amazon category
  Size Tier       → Match your product's size/weight class
  Unit Weight     → Weight in lbs
  Product Cost    → Your cost per unit (landed)
  Prep Cost       → Any prep/labeling cost per unit
  Inbound Ship    → Cost to ship one unit to FBA
  Selling Price   → Your Amazon listing price
  Monthly Volume  → How many units you expect to sell/month
  Storage Volume  → Cubic feet per unit (check your box dimensions)
  Storage Month   → Toggle Jan-Sep or Oct-Dec (peak season is higher)
  Target ROI      → Slide to your desired return on investment

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 3 — READ YOUR RESULTS
─────────────────────────────────────────
Results update instantly as you type:

  Net Profit     → Profit per unit after all fees and costs
  Margin         → % of selling price you keep
  ROI            → Return on your total cost
  Amazon's Cut   → Total FBA fees (referral + fulfillment + storage)
  Monthly Profit → Net profit × monthly volume

Check the profitability badge:
  STRONG  → Margin > 40%  ✓
  HEALTHY → Margin 20-40% ✓
  THIN    → Margin < 20%  ⚠
  LOSS    → Losing money  ✗

─────────────────────────────────────────
STEP 4 — SAVE YOUR PRODUCT
─────────────────────────────────────────
Click "Save Product" or press Ctrl + S.
View all saved products under 02 products.

─────────────────────────────────────────
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
─────────────────────────────────────────
  Ctrl + S  →  Save product
  Ctrl + E  →  Export all products to CSV
  Ctrl + K  →  Focus search bar
  Ctrl + P  →  Print current view
  Escape    →  Close any modal
