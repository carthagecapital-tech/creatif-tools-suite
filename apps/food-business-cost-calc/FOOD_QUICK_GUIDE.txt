  FOOD BUSINESS COST CALCULATOR — QUICK GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1 — COST A RECIPE
────────────────────────
Go to the Recipe Calculator tab (01).

Fill in:
  • Recipe Name and Category
  • Batch Yield         (how many units this recipe makes)
  • Ingredients         (click "Add Ingredient" for each one)
    - Purchase quantity + price paid
    - Recipe quantity used
    - Yield loss %       (waste from peeling, trimming, etc.)
  • Hours to make the batch
  • Hourly Rate         (what you want to earn per hour)
  • Packaging cost per unit
  • Kitchen overhead for the batch
  • Booth Fee           (if selling at a farmers market)
  • Expected units sold at the event
  • Round trip miles to market

Use "I want to make" to scale the batch up or down —
all ingredient quantities and costs rescale automatically.

Adjust the Target Margin % slider to see how different
pricing affects your profit and true hourly wage.

Results update in real time:
  Cost Per Unit / Suggested Price / Net Profit /
  Profit Per Unit / Margin % / True Hourly Wage /
  Break-Even Price

Click "Save Recipe" (or Ctrl + S) to store it.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 2 — COMPARE RECIPES
──────────────────────────
Go to the Compare tab (03).

Load two saved recipes or set up two scenarios manually
and see cost, profit, margin, and true hourly wage
side-by-side — useful for comparing batch sizes or
testing different pricing strategies.

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 3 — REVIEW SAVED RECIPES
────────────────────────────────
Go to Saved Recipes (02).

  • Filter by category (Baked Goods, Jams, Sauces, etc.)
  • Sort any column
  • Export to CSV (Ctrl + E)
  • Edit or delete entries

Each recipe displays a profitability badge:
  Strong      — Margin 40%+
  Healthy     — Margin 20-40%
  Low Margin  — Margin under 20%
  Underpriced — True wage below 80% of your target rate

─────────────────────────────────────────────────────

STEP 4 — CHECK ANALYTICS
──────────────────────────
Go to Analytics (04) for a visual breakdown of margins,
true hourly wages, and cost composition across all
saved recipes.
