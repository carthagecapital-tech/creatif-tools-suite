========================================
 POD Profit Calculator Pro
 QUICK GUIDE – Get Started in 2 Minutes
========================================

1. OPEN THE APP
   Double‑click POD_Profit_Calculator.html. The Calculator view appears.

2. ENTER YOUR PRODUCT
   • Name: e.g., "Vintage Cat Tee"
   • Product Type: T‑Shirt, Hoodie, Mug, etc.
   • Supplier: Printify, Printful, Gelato, or Manual
   • Base Cost: Auto‑filled from presets; adjust if needed
   • Selling Channel: Etsy, Shopify, Direct
   • Selling Price: Your retail price
   • Shipping Cost: Per‑unit shipping (usually $4–$6)
   • Shipping Paid By: Toggle whether you or the buyer pays

3. SEE RESULTS
   The Results cards update live:
   • Net Profit per unit
   • Profit Margin %
   • Markup %
   • Total Fees breakdown (bar chart)
   • Break‑Even Price (minimum to cover costs)
   • Suggested Price (to hit your target margin)

4. ADJUST YOUR TARGET MARGIN
   Use the slider to set your desired profit margin. The Suggested Price will change accordingly.

5. SAVE A PRODUCT
   Click "Save Product" (or press Ctrl+S). The product appears in the Saved Products view.

6. COMPARE SCENARIOS
   • Fill in a product in the Calculator, then click "Send to Compare".
   • Switch to the Compare view (nav 03) to see side‑by‑side numbers.

7. MANAGE YOUR LIST
   Go to Saved Products (nav 02). You can:
   • Search, sort, filter by supplier or profitability
   • Select multiple products and delete them
   • Load any product back into the Calculator
   • Duplicate or delete individual items
   • Export all saved data as CSV (Ctrl+E)

8. CHECK ANALYTICS
   The Analytics view (nav 04) shows bar charts, a cost breakdown donut, and summary stats.

9. CUSTOMISE SETTINGS
   In Settings (nav 05) you can change defaults, accent colour, manage custom suppliers, export/import full JSON backups, and clear all data.

10. LEARN MORE
    Open Help (nav 06) for formula references, fee tables, keyboard shortcuts, and privacy info.

That’s it! You’re ready to price your POD products with confidence.
