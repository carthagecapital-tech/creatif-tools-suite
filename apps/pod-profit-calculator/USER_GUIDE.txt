1. OVERVIEW
   POD Profit Calculator Pro is a browser‑based tool for print‑on‑demand sellers. It calculates net profit, fees, break‑even price, and suggested retail price based on product type, supplier, selling channel, and shipping details. You can save products, compare two configurations side‑by‑side, view analytics charts, and export data for reporting.

   All data is stored locally – nothing leaves your device.

2. INSTALLATION & LAUNCHING
   No installation required. Simply open the POD_Profit_Calculator.html file in a modern web browser:
   • Double‑click the file, or
   • Drag it onto an open browser window.

   Supported browsers: Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, Mozilla Firefox, Apple Safari (latest versions recommended).

   The app works offline after the initial page load; you do not need an internet connection.

3. INTERFACE TOUR
   The app consists of:
   • Sidebar (left) – logo, global search, navigation menu, live summary, and user status.
   • Main area – header with view title and action buttons, content canvas with the active view, and a footer.
   • Toast notifications – appear at the bottom right.
   • Modal dialogs – used for confirmations and data input.

   Use the sidebar links (01‑06) to switch between views. The active view is highlighted.

4. CALCULATOR VIEW (01)
   This is the primary workspace.

   Form Fields:
   • Product Name – free text (used for saved product identification).
   • Product Type – select from T‑Shirt, Hoodie, Mug, Poster, Tote Bag, Phone Case, Canvas Print, Sticker Sheet.
   • Supplier – choose Printify, Printful, Gelato, Manual, or any custom supplier you’ve added (Settings).
   • Base Cost – the production cost per unit. Presets auto‑fill for non‑Manual suppliers but can be overridden.
   • Selling Channel – Etsy, Shopify, or Direct.
   • Selling Price – your retail price.
   • Shipping Cost – the amount you or the buyer pay for shipping.
   • Shipping Paid By – toggle between Buyer and Seller.
   • Target Margin – slider (10‑80%) used for the Suggested Price calculation.

   Results Cards:
   • Net Profit – Selling Price minus all costs. Green pill = profit, red = loss.
   • Profit Margin – Net Profit as a percentage of Selling Price.
   • Markup – (Selling Price − Base Cost) / Base Cost × 100.
   • Total Fees – sum of platform, processing, listing, and seller‑paid shipping.
   • Break‑Even Price – minimum price to exactly cover all costs.
   • Suggested Price – price that achieves your Target Margin.

   Fee Breakdown Bar: Visual representation of where your money goes.

   Action Buttons:
   • Reset Form – clears all fields (with confirmation).
   • Send to Compare – copies current calculator settings to the Compare view (column A).
   • Save Product – saves the current product with all calculated results.

5. SAVED PRODUCTS VIEW (02)
   Displays all products you’ve saved in a table.

   Features:
   • Search box filters by name, type, or supplier.
   • Filter chips: All, by supplier, Profitable (net profit > 0), Loss (≤ 0).
   • Sort columns by clicking headers (click again to reverse).
   • Select all / individual checkboxes for bulk delete.
   • Row actions: Load (back to Calculator), Copy (duplicate), Delete.
   • Export CSV – downloads all saved products as a spreadsheet‑friendly file.
   • Pagination at the bottom for large datasets.

6. COMPARE VIEW (03)
   Side‑by‑side comparison of two product configurations (A and B).

   • Each column has its own form fields.
   • Results update in real time as you type.
   • A winner banner shows which configuration nets more profit per sale.
   • You can pre‑fill column A from the Calculator using the "Send to Compare" button.

7. ANALYTICS VIEW (04)
   Provides charts and summary statistics for all saved products.

   • Bar Chart – Net profit per product (last 15 items).
   • Donut Chart – Aggregate cost breakdown (base, platform, processing, shipping).
   • Summary Grid – total revenue, total costs, total net profit, average margin, best/worst product.

8. SETTINGS VIEW (05)
   Customise the app to your workflow.

   Sections:
   • Default Values – Supplier, Channel, Target Margin, Shipping Cost.
   • Appearance – Accent hue slider (changes the orange highlight colour).
   • Custom Suppliers – Add your own suppliers with commission and processing rates.
   • Data Management – Export/import JSON backups, and clear all data permanently.

   Changes are saved automatically when you adjust a setting.

9. HELP VIEW (06)
   Reference information for understanding the calculations.

   • Formula Reference – all mathematical formulas used.
   • Fees by Selling Channel – Etsy, Shopify, Direct fee structures.
   • Typical Base Costs by Supplier – approximate product costs for Printify, Printful, Gelato.
   • Keyboard Shortcuts – list of useful key combinations.
   • Tips for Maximum Profit – best practices.
   • Privacy & Legal – data storage information.

10. DATA MANAGEMENT & BACKUP
    • CSV Export: In Saved Products view, click "Export CSV" to download a spreadsheet. The file is safe from formula injection (strings are properly escaped).
    • JSON Backup: In Settings, click "Export JSON" to save all products, settings, and custom suppliers as a .json file.
    • Import Backup: Use the file picker and "Import" button in Settings to merge a previous backup. Imported products are sanitized; malformed data will be coerced to valid numbers.
    • Clear All Data: Removes everything permanently (with confirmation).

11. KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
    • Ctrl + S  → Save current product (from Calculator)
    • Ctrl + K  → Focus the search box
    • Ctrl + E  → Export CSV (in Saved Products view) or Export JSON (in Settings view)
    • Ctrl + P  → Print current view
    • Escape    → Close any open modal dialog

12. PRIVACY & LEGAL
    All your data is stored locally using browser localStorage. No personal or product data is transmitted over the internet. The app does not use cookies or any form of tracking.

    For help or questions, contact the seller through Etsy messages.
