§ 01Formula Reference

Platform Fee = Selling Price × Transaction %

Processing Fee = Selling Price × Processing % + Fixed Fee

Listing Fee = Fixed per-sale fee (Etsy only)

Channel Fees = Platform Fee + Processing Fee + Listing Fee

Seller Shipping = Shipping Cost (if seller pays) or $0 (if buyer pays)

Total Fees = Channel Fees + Seller Shipping

Total Costs = Base Cost + Total Fees

Gross Profit = Selling Price − Base Cost

Net Profit = Selling Price − Total Costs

Profit Margin = (Net Profit ÷ Selling Price) × 100

Markup % = ((Selling Price − Base Cost) ÷ Base Cost) × 100

Break-Even Price = (Base Cost + Fixed Fees + Shipping) ÷ (1 − Variable Rate)

Suggested Price = (Base Cost + Fixed Fees + Shipping) ÷ (1 − Variable Rate − Target Margin)

§ 05Tips for Maximum Profit

  • Compare multiple supplier+channel combinations before committing to a product line
  • Factor in shipping costs even when the buyer pays — it affects conversion rates
  • Etsy’s 6.5% transaction fee is significant — consider Shopify for higher-volume sales
  • Use the target margin slider to find the sweet spot between profit and competitiveness
  • Save products with different pricing scenarios to compare later
  • Monitor actual costs monthly — supplier pricing changes affect your margins

§ 06Privacy & Support

Data Privacy: All product data is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No information is ever sent to the internet or shared with any third party.

Supported Browsers: This tool is optimized for modern Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari. Keep your browser updated for the best experience.
