Why these apps

Most health apps want to integrate with your doctor's office, sync to the cloud, or sell anonymized data. These apps do the opposite: you enter your own data, see your trends, and bring the journal to your next appointment if you want to share it. Local-first, private, simple.

How to choose the right app

  • Log labs as soon as you get them. Reference ranges change between labs, so capture the units too.
  • For medications, track the start and stop dates along with dose — it makes side-effect correlations easier later.
  • Bring the journal to your doctor. Most appreciate seeing the trends you've tracked over time.

Frequently asked questions

Is my health data private?
Yes. 100% local. Nothing leaves your device. The app is not HIPAA-regulated because it doesn't touch a server.
Can I import data from my doctor's office?
Not directly. You can copy numbers from your patient portal into the journal manually.
