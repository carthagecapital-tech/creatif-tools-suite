Why these apps

Most resellers underestimate their true per-item profit. After eBay fees, Etsy transaction fees, Offsite Ads, PayPal fees, shipping supplies, and ad spend, a 'good' sale can actually be a money-loser. The apps here do that math for you, instantly. They're local-first (your inventory and financials stay private on your device), and they don't take a cut of your sales.

How to choose the right app

  • Start with a profit calculator — plug in your typical eBay/Etsy/Amazon fees and see what you actually make per item.
  • Add inventory tracking once you're listing 50+ items per month — track cost, sale price, fees, and net profit per SKU.
  • If you sell on multiple platforms, a multi-platform profit calculator helps you compare which channel is most profitable.
  • For POD (print-on-demand) sellers, a POD-specific profit calculator accounts for production costs and platform fees.

Frequently asked questions

Which platforms do these apps support?
The reseller apps cover eBay, Etsy, Poshmark, Mercari, Amazon FBA, Shopify, and Merch by Amazon. Some are platform-specific (Amazon FBA only) and others are multi-platform.
Do these apps connect to eBay/Etsy/Amazon APIs?
No. They're local-first single-file HTML apps, so all data stays on your device and never touches a third-party API. This is more private and works offline, but you'll enter your sales data manually or paste from the platform.
Do I have to enter every transaction?
For the calculators, yes — you enter buy price, sale price, and the app does the math. For inventory tracking, you enter items as you source them. Most resellers spend 5-10 minutes a day on this and get clear visibility into what's making money.
