Why these apps

Most wedding apps either charge a fortune (full-service planners) or are glorified spreadsheets with a paywall (The Knot, WeddingWire). This app does the same job, locally, for a one-time fee. Drag-and-drop seating, multi-vendor budget, auto-PDF printables — all the essentials without the bloat.

How to choose the right app

  • Start with your guest list and budget before anything else. Everything else flows from those two numbers.
  • Use the drag-and-drop seating chart to test different arrangements. Move tables around in seconds, not with sticky notes on a wall.
  • Print or PDF the timeline and seating chart for your venue coordinator a week before the wedding.

Frequently asked questions

Does it have RSVP tracking?
The app focuses on planning, not guest-facing RSVP. For RSVPs, most couples use a free tool like Google Forms.
Can multiple people access the same wedding?
The app is local-first, so you'd need to share the file (e.g. via Dropbox) with your partner or wedding party to collaborate.
