What is Bill of Sale Pro?

Bill of Sale Pro is a state-aware vehicle bill of sale generator that produces print-ready, legally-sound documents for private party sales. Whether you're selling a car, motorcycle, boat, RV, trailer, or horse, the app walks you through every required field and pre-fills the right state-specific disclosures.

The federal odometer disclosure is built in (required for vehicles under 10 years old), and the app tracks 12 states with full disclosure data — California, New York, Florida, Texas, Illinois, Ohio, Pennsylvania, Washington, Georgia, North Carolina, Arizona, and Michigan. For the remaining 38 states, the app shows a verify-with-DMV notice so you're never misled about what's required.

Unlike online bill-of-sale templates that require uploading your data or paying monthly fees, Bill of Sale Pro runs entirely in your browser. All your data stays on your device. No account. No subscription. No tracking.

Vehicle types supported

Each vehicle type has its own conditional fields — boats need the Hull Identification Number, horses need breed and registration info, trailers need GVWR, and so on.

Horse
Breed, age, registration, microchip #

Frequently asked questions

Does it work for all 50 states?
Yes. Bill of Sale Pro covers all 50 states plus DC. 12 states (CA, NY, FL, TX, IL, OH, PA, WA, GA, NC, AZ, MI) include full disclosure data. The remaining 38 states show a verify-with-DMV notice so you're never misled about what's required.
What vehicle types are supported?
Car, motorcycle, trailer, boat (with HIN), RV, horse (with breed/age), and general equipment. Each vehicle type has its own conditional fields — for example, boats require the Hull Identification Number (HIN), and horses require breed and registration info.
Does it include the federal odometer disclosure?
Yes. The federal odometer disclosure is required for vehicles under 10 years old, and Bill of Sale Pro includes it automatically. The disclosure tracks the odometer reading at time of sale, accuracy declaration, and both buyer and seller signatures.
Can I save my work and come back?
Yes. The app auto-saves to your browser every time you change a field. You can also save named drafts (up to 5) and load them later. Your data never leaves your device.
How do I print or save as PDF?
Press ⌘P (or click 'Print / PDF' in the topbar). The app strips all the UI chrome and prints a clean, single-page bill of sale ready for signatures. Most browsers let you 'Save as PDF' from the print dialog.
