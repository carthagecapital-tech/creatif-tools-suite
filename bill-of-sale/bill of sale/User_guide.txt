1. OPENING THE APP
================================================================================

BillOfSale Pro is a single HTML file. There is nothing to install.

  - Double-click billofsale-pro.html. It opens in your default browser.
  - If it opens in a text editor instead, right-click the file, choose
    "Open With", and pick your browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).

The app works on:
  - macOS (any version from the last 5 years)
  - Windows (Windows 10 and 11)
  - Linux (Ubuntu, Fedora, etc.)
  - ChromeOS
  - iPhone and iPad (Safari)
  - Android phones and tablets (Chrome)

The app needs a modern browser. If your browser is more than 3 years old,
update it for the best experience.

After the first open, you do NOT need an internet connection. The app
runs entirely on your device.

2. THE INTERFACE
================================================================================

The app has four areas:

  SIDEBAR (left)
    Brand mark, search trigger, vehicle type list, system actions
    (drafts, reset, help). Click any vehicle type to switch.

  TOPBAR (top)
    Breadcrumb showing where you are, page title, state selector,
    save draft button, print/PDF button.

  VEHICLE TABS (just below the topbar)
    Same as the sidebar vehicle list - shown as horizontal tabs.
    Useful on mobile when the sidebar collapses.

  WORKSPACE (main area, two columns)
    LEFT: the fillable form
    RIGHT: the live bill of sale document preview

On screens wider than 1100 pixels, both columns are side by side. On
smaller screens (tablets, phones), the form appears first and the preview
is below it. A floating "Preview" button in the bottom-right corner of
mobile scrolls to the preview when tapped.

3. VEHICLE TYPES AND TYPE-SPECIFIC FIELDS
================================================================================

Seven vehicle types are supported. Each has its own field set under
section 01 "Vehicle". The type-specific fields adapt automatically.

  CAR
    VIN, year, make, model, color, odometer, odometer status,
    federal odometer disclosure, title number, body style, license
    plate, lienholder (optional).

  MOTORCYCLE
    VIN, engine number, year, make, model, color, odometer,
    engine displacement (cc), title number.

  TRAILER
    VIN, GVWR (gross vehicle weight rating), axle count, trailer type
    (utility, cargo, boat, travel, livestock, flatbed, horse),
    length, empty weight, title number.

  BOAT
    HIN (Hull Identification Number), year, make, model, length,
    hull material, propulsion type, engine details,
    trailer included (optional sub-section), title number.

  RV
    VIN, year, make, model, class (A, B, C, travel trailer,
    fifth wheel, pop-up), length, odometer, sleeps-how-many,
    title number.

  HORSE
    Animal name, breed, age, sex, color/markings, height (in hands),
    registration/microchip number (optional), health certificate date
    (optional), brand/tattoo (optional), tack included (checkboxes).

  EQUIPMENT (GENERAL)
    Item description, make, model, serial number, year (optional),
    condition (new/used/refurbished), hours of use (optional),
    manual included, warranty remaining.

To switch vehicle type, click any item in the sidebar or the horizontal
tabs. The vehicle section updates immediately. Other sections (seller,
buyer, sale, etc.) keep their values.

NOTE: switching vehicle type does NOT erase your seller, buyer, or sale
data. Only the vehicle-specific fields change.

4. FILLING THE COMMON SECTIONS
================================================================================

Sections 02 (Seller), 03 (Buyer), 04 (Sale), and 06 (Signatures) are the
same for all vehicle types.

  SELLER (Section 02)
    Full legal name, street address, city, ZIP, phone (optional),
    email (optional).

  BUYER (Section 03)
    Same fields as Seller.

  SALE (Section 04)
    Sale date (defaults to today).
    Sale price in US dollars (numeric, no commas).
    Payment method (cash, check, money order, wire, PayPal, Venmo,
    Zelle, or other).
    Gift transfer checkbox (see Section 6).
    Trade-in involved checkbox (see Section 6).

  STATE DISCLOSURES (Section 05)
    Auto-populated when you select a state. See Section 5 below.

  SIGNATURES (Section 06)
    Notarize toggle (see Section 5).
    Witness toggle (see Section 7).

All required fields are marked with a red asterisk (*).

================================================================================
5. STATE DISCLOSURES AND NOTARIZATION
================================================================================

Twelve US states carry pre-filled state-specific disclosure data:

  California, New York, Florida, Texas, Illinois, Ohio, Pennsylvania,
  Washington, Georgia, North Carolina, Arizona, Michigan.

For these states, Section 05 of the form shows the relevant disclosures
as checkboxes or notices. The disclosure clauses also appear inline in
the printed bill of sale.

For all other states (38 remaining US states + DC), the form shows a
verification notice. You should still confirm requirements with your
state DMV before finalizing.

NOTARIZATION
------------
Three states require notarization of a vehicle bill of sale:

  Florida (FL)
  Pennsylvania (PA)
  North Carolina (NC)

When you select one of these states, the "Include notary block" toggle
under Section 06 (Signatures) is automatically enabled. The notary block
also appears in the printed bill of sale.

The notary block includes:
  - State and county lines
  - Subscribed-and-sworn statement with date
  - Notary signature line and commission expiration
  - Visual placeholder for the notary seal

You can manually toggle the notary block on or off for any state if you
have a specific reason (e.g., voluntary notarization in a state that
doesn't require it).

6. SPECIAL TRANSACTIONS
================================================================================

GIFT TRANSFER
-------------
When the buyer is a family member and no money changes hands:

  1. Fill in seller and buyer sections normally.
  2. Under Sale (Section 04), check the "Gift transfer" box.
  3. Leave the sale price field empty or enter 0.
  4. The bill of sale renders with price "$0.00 - Gift".

Some states require an additional "gift letter" for tax purposes. Check
your state's DMV rules.

TRADE-IN
--------
When the buyer is trading in their old vehicle as part of the payment:

  1. Fill in seller and buyer sections normally.
  2. Under Sale (Section 04), check the "Trade-in involved" box.
  3. A sub-section appears asking for the trade-in vehicle:
     - Trade-in vehicle VIN (or description if no VIN)
     - Trade-in vehicle description (year/make/model)
     - Trade-in value (the dollar amount credited toward this sale)
  4. The bill of sale includes the trade-in details and shows the net
     sale price (sale price minus trade-in value).

7. SIGNATURES AND WITNESS LINES
================================================================================

After printing the bill of sale, both seller and buyer sign with a pen.
For high-value transactions or per state requirements, a witness or
notary may also sign.

WITNESS SIGNATURE
-----------------
For sales over a few thousand dollars, or for any transaction where you
want extra legal protection, have a neutral third party witness the
signatures. Toggle "Include witness signature line" under Section 06.
The witness line appears in the printed document.

NOTARY ACKNOWLEDGMENT
----------------------
Required in Florida, Pennsylvania, and North Carolina for vehicle bill
of sale. Auto-enabled when you select one of those states. See Section 5.

To use the notary block:
  1. Print the bill of sale with the notary block visible.
  2. Both seller and buyer sign in the presence of a notary public.
  3. The notary fills in state, county, date, signs, dates, and applies
     their official seal.

Most banks, UPS stores, and shipping centers offer notary services for
a small fee (typically $5-$15 per signature).

8. SAVING AND LOADING DRAFTS
================================================================================

BillOfSale Pro keeps up to 5 saved drafts in your browser's localStorage.

SAVING A DRAFT
--------------
  - Click "Save draft" in the top bar (or press Ctrl/Cmd + S).
  - The current form state is captured (all fields, vehicle type, state).
  - The status bar at the bottom shows "draft saved" with the current time.
  - If you already have 5 drafts, the oldest is automatically removed.

VIEWING AND LOADING DRAFTS
--------------------------
  - Click the "drafts" item in the sidebar (or press Ctrl/Cmd + K and
    search for a draft).
  - The Drafts modal shows your saved drafts sorted by date (newest first).
  - Each draft shows vehicle type, state, and a preview (VIN, seller name,
    or "Empty form" if you haven't filled much yet).
  - Click "Load" to load a draft into the app. Your current form state is
    REPLACED with the draft.
  - Click "Delete" to permanently remove a draft (asks first).

AUTOSAVE
--------
The current form is auto-saved to localStorage every time you change a
field (debounced 500ms). This is different from a "draft" - autosave is
always the latest state, while drafts are snapshots you explicitly save.

If you close the browser and reopen the file later, your form is exactly
where you left it.

9. PRINTING AND SAVING AS PDF
================================================================================

The print output is a clean, single-page (or multi-page if needed) legal
document. All app interface elements (sidebar, topbar, form fields,
buttons) are hidden in print. Only the bill of sale document itself
prints.

TO PRINT ON PAPER:
  1. Connect your printer.
  2. Click "Print / PDF" in the top bar (or press Ctrl/Cmd + P).
  3. In your browser's print dialog, select your printer.
  4. Click Print.

TO SAVE AS PDF:
  1. Click "Print / PDF" (or press Ctrl/Cmd + P).
  2. In your browser's print dialog, change the "Destination" to
     "Save as PDF" (Chrome/Edge) or use "PDF > Save as PDF" (Safari).
  3. Choose where to save the file.
  4. Click Save.

PRINT FOOTER:
-------------
Every printed page includes a footer with:
  - Copyright notice (C 2026 BillOfSale Pro - All Rights Reserved)
  - Generation date (the date you printed)
  - Page numbers (Page 1, Page 2, etc.)

This is standard for legal documents and helps establish provenance.

PAGE SIZE:
----------
The print stylesheet uses US Letter (8.5 x 11 inches). If you're in a
country that uses A4, you can change the page size in your browser's
print dialog under "More settings" or "Paper size".

10. CSV EXPORT
================================================================================

For users handling multiple transactions (small dealers, fleet managers,
estate executors), the CSV export saves the current form as one row in
a spreadsheet-friendly format.

  1. Fill in the form for one transaction.
  2. Press Ctrl/Cmd + E (or find Export in the menu - if implemented).
  3. A CSV file downloads with all the form fields as columns.

To export multiple transactions: fill, export, clear form (Ctrl/Cmd + R),
repeat. Each CSV file represents one transaction.

The CSV file can be opened in Excel, Google Sheets, Numbers, or any
spreadsheet app.

NOTE: CSV export saves the current form state only. It does NOT save
drafts. To preserve a transaction for later editing, save it as a draft
(Ctrl/Cmd + S).

11. LIGHT AND DARK MODE
================================================================================

The app supports both light and dark themes.

  - The default matches your operating system preference (detected on
    first open).
  - Click the theme toggle button in the top-right corner of the app
    (looks like a moon or sun) to switch manually.
  - Or press Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + L.
  - Your choice is remembered for next time.

For printing, the app automatically switches to light mode (white
background, black text) regardless of your theme setting. This produces
the cleanest printed output and saves printer ink.

12. KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
================================================================================

  Ctrl/Cmd + K          Open search palette
  Ctrl/Cmd + S          Save current form as a draft
  Ctrl/Cmd + P          Print or save as PDF
  Ctrl/Cmd + E          Export current form as CSV
  Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R  Reset form (asks first)
  Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + L  Toggle light/dark theme
  ?                     Open help dialog
  Esc                   Close any open dialog

The app also supports standard browser shortcuts (Ctrl/Cmd + T for new
tab, Ctrl/Cmd + W to close tab, etc.).

On macOS, "Cmd" is used. On Windows and Linux, "Ctrl" is used.

13. TROUBLESHOOTING
================================================================================

THE FILE OPENS IN A TEXT EDITOR INSTEAD OF A BROWSER
  - Right-click billofsale-pro.html, choose "Open With", pick your browser.
  - On Windows: you may need to check "Always use this app" the first time.

THE PRINT DIALOG DOESN'T SHOW "SAVE AS PDF"
  - You're probably using a very old browser. Update Chrome, Edge, or
    Firefox to the latest version.
  - On Safari (macOS), use the PDF dropdown at the bottom of the print
    dialog.

MY FORM DATA DISAPPEARED
  - BillOfSale Pro stores data in your browser's localStorage. If you:
    * Cleared your browser data/history
    * Used "Private" or "Incognito" browsing mode
    * Switched browsers
  - Your drafts and autosaved form are gone. This is by design - your
    data stays on your device, not on any server.

THE LIVE PREVIEW IS NOT UPDATING
  - Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser. The app needs it
    for the live preview and state disclosures.
  - Try refreshing the page (F5 or Ctrl/Cmd + R).

THE BILL OF SALE LOOKS WRONG IN PRINT
  - Make sure your browser's print scale is set to 100% (not "Fit to
    page"). The document is already sized for US Letter.

A SPECIFIC STATE'S DISCLOSURE IS NOT SHOWING
  - Only 12 states have full pre-filled disclosures (see Section 5).
    For other states, the app shows a verification notice. You should
    confirm requirements with your state DMV.

================================================================================
14. DATA PRIVACY AND STORAGE
================================================================================

BillOfSale Pro does NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data
on any server. All your data stays on your device.

What's stored on your device (in browser localStorage):
  - Your current form state (autosaved)
  - Your saved drafts (up to 5)
  - Your theme preference (light or dark)
  - Your last selected vehicle type and state

What's NOT stored or transmitted:
  - No analytics
  - No cookies
  - No tracking pixels
  - No account creation
  - No email collection
  - No server-side processing

To permanently delete all stored data:
  - Open the app, click "reset" in the sidebar (or Ctrl/Cmd + Shift + R)
  - Confirm the reset
  - All form data, drafts, and preferences are erased

Or:
  - Clear your browser's localStorage for this file's URL
  - Or clear all browser data (this affects other sites too)
