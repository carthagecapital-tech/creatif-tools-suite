--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
HOW TO OPEN
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. Save billofsale-pro.html anywhere on your computer.
  2. Double-click the file. It will open in your default browser
     (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge - any modern browser works).
  3. The app loads instantly. No installation. No login. No internet needed
     after this file is saved.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE 60-SECOND VERSION
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - Pick your vehicle type from the left sidebar (car, motorcycle, trailer,
    boat, RV, horse, or equipment).
  - Pick your state from the dropdown in the top right.
  - Fill in the form on the left. The bill of sale builds live on the right.
  - When done, click "Print / PDF" in the top bar (or press Ctrl/Cmd + P).
    Choose "Save as PDF" in your browser's print dialog.
  - Sign it. Share it. Done.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
IMPORTANT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  BillOfSale Pro is a self-serve tool, not legal advice. It produces a
  printable bill of sale form with state-specific disclosures for the 12
  states listed in the app. For all other states, it produces a general
  form and reminds you to verify requirements with your state DMV.

  If you have questions about your specific transaction, contact a licensed
  attorney or your state's Department of Motor Vehicles.
