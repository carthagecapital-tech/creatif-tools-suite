How to use BillOfSale Pro
  1. Pick a vehicle type. Use the sidebar or the tab strip — car, motorcycle, trailer, boat, RV, horse, or equipment. The form fields below adjust automatically.
  2. Pick a state. Use the selector in the top-right. State-specific disclosures and notarization requirements update automatically.
  3. Fill in the form. Vehicle details, seller, buyer, sale terms, and signatures. The document on the right updates live as you type.
  4. Save a draft anytime with ⌘S — your last 5 drafts are kept and can be reloaded from the sidebar.
  5. Print or save as PDF with ⌘P — only the clean document prints, with no app chrome.
