WHAT IS THIS
------------
  A printable, state-aware vehicle bill of sale generator. One file,
  runs in any browser, no installation. Works offline.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
THE 4 STEPS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  1. PICK VEHICLE TYPE
     Sidebar on the left or the tab strip across the top.
     7 types: car, motorcycle, trailer, boat, RV, horse, equipment.

  2. PICK STATE
     Dropdown in the top right. 50 US states + DC.
     The form auto-loads state-specific disclosures (CA, NY, FL, TX, IL,
     OH, PA, WA, GA, NC, AZ, MI have full disclosure data; other states
     show a verification notice).

  3. FILL THE FORM
     Six sections, marked with section numbers:
       01 Vehicle    (type-specific fields)
       02 Seller     (name, address, phone, email)
       03 Buyer      (same fields)
       04 Sale       (date, price, payment method)
       05 State      (auto-loaded, edit if needed)
       06 Signatures (notarize + witness toggles)
     Required fields are marked with a red asterisk (*).
     The bill of sale on the right updates live as you type.

  4. PRINT OR SAVE AS PDF
     Click "Print / PDF" in the top bar OR press Ctrl/Cmd + P.
     In the print dialog, choose "Save as PDF" as the destination.
     Only the clean document prints - no app interface.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
KEYBOARD SHORTCUTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  Ctrl/Cmd + K    Open search (jump to vehicle type, state, or draft)
  Ctrl/Cmd + S    Save current form as a draft (keeps last 5)
  Ctrl/Cmd + P    Print or save as PDF
  Ctrl/Cmd + E    Export current form as CSV (one row per sale)
  Ctrl/Cmd + R    Reset form (asks first)
  Ctrl/Cmd + L    Toggle light/dark theme
  ?               Open this help dialog
  Esc             Close any open dialog

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
COMMON WORKFLOWS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  STANDARD PRIVATE SALE
    Pick vehicle type > pick state > fill seller/buyer/sale > print PDF >
    sign with pen.

  GIFT TRANSFER (FAMILY)
    Same as above but check the "Gift transfer" box under Sale details.
    The price renders as "$0.00 - Gift" in the printed document.

  TRADE-IN AT A DEALER
    Pick vehicle type > check "Trade-in involved" under Sale details >
    fill in the trade-in vehicle's VIN, description, and trade value.

  NOTARIZED SALE (FL, PA, NC REQUIRE THIS)
    Pick one of those states - the notary block auto-appears in the
    preview and prints in the document. Bring the printed bill of sale
    to a notary public to complete.

  HIGH-VALUE TRANSACTION
    Toggle "Include witness signature line" under Signatures. Have a
    neutral third party sign as witness.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  All form data, drafts, and theme preference are stored in your browser's
  localStorage. Nothing is uploaded. Nothing is sent to any server.
  Clearing your browser data will erase saved drafts.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
WHAT'S NOT IN THIS APP
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

  - It does NOT submit your bill of sale to your state DMV. You'll need
    your state's title transfer form for that (links provided in the
    app under the state-specific disclosures).
  - It does NOT generate a smog certificate or emissions test result.
  - It does NOT replace legal advice for unusual transactions (lien
    sales, estate sales with multiple heirs, etc.).
