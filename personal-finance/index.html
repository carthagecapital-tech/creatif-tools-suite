Why these apps

Most personal finance apps want to link to your bank, store your data in their cloud, and charge you $10/month for the privilege. These apps do the opposite: all data stays on your device, no account needed, no bank linking required. You enter your numbers, the app does the math, you get clarity.

How to choose the right app

  • Start with the debt payoff calculator if you have any high-interest debt — it projects your payoff timeline so you can see the light at the end of the tunnel.
  • Track your net worth monthly. Even small monthly improvements compound over years.
  • For precious metals investors, a dedicated tracker gives you cost basis and unrealized gains without the spreadsheet hassle.
