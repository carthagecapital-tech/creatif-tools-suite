Why these apps

Each app in this collection was designed for the realities of running a cleaning business: tight schedules, multiple properties per day, the need to send professional quotes and reports to clients, and the constant juggle between new leads and recurring service. They're local-first (your data stays on your device), require no account, and work offline. Buy once, use forever.

How to choose the right app

  • Start with a quote calculator if you're still onboarding new clients — fast, professional quotes win more jobs.
  • Add a route planner once you have 3+ recurring clients — it saves gas and lets you fit more jobs per day.
  • Use an inspection app for quality control on big commercial accounts — printable reports impress clients.
  • A forms bundle covers client intake, service agreements, and work orders without a subscription.
  • If you manage multiple crews, a job scheduler with start/stop time tracking is a no-brainer.
