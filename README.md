## The post

**Title:** Cleaning Business Pricing Guide: How to Set Your Rates for Profit (2026)

**Target keywords:** how to price cleaning services, cleaning business pricing, cleaning service rates, residential cleaning pricing, commercial cleaning rates

**Word count:** ~3,300 words

**Structure:**
1. Why most cleaners underprice
2. Hourly vs flat-rate pricing
3. Calculate your true hourly cost (with formula)
4. Set your pricing tiers (with rate tables)
5. The flat-rate formula
6. Quote in under 5 minutes
7. When to raise prices
8. Track and adjust monthly
9. Common pricing mistakes
10. 30-day action plan
11. FAQ (5 questions with schema)

**SEO features:**
- Article schema (with author, datePublished, articleSection, keywords)
- FAQPage schema (5 questions, eligible for FAQ rich results)
- BreadcrumbList schema
- Internal links to 8+ cleaning apps
- 2 data tables (rate tables for residential + commercial)
- 8 tool cards linking to individual app landing pages
- 1 quote/formula callout
- 1 ordered list (30-day action plan)
- 1 FAQ section with rich schema
- Plausible analytics tracking
- Multi-format favicon links
- Optimized meta description targeting keywords

**Internal linking structure (funnel readers to apps):**
- Step 4 (quote in 5 min) -> 3 app cards (CleanQuote, CleaningPricer, CleanForms)
- Step 6 (when to raise) -> link to cleanforms-pro
- "Tools that make this easier" section -> 8 app cards
- FAQ -> links to cleanquote-pro
- Related section -> 6 internal links
